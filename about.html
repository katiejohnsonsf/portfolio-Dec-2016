HELLO AGAIN!

In 2010 Katie was working as an analyst at 3Degrees — an environmental commodities trading firm. Having followered her interest in the environment to a degree in environmental science at Mills, she was frustrated with the structural barriers she faced within a company ostensibly oriented towards affecting structural change in the energy market.

When an Airbnb guest of her roommate’s engaged her in conversation in her kitchen one morning, Katie was awestruck from the experience of being shown a new world through the conversation. Katie wanted others to have this experience.

Katie sought out companies that were enabling these kind of interactions. Airbnb had brought her the world-expanding conversation, so she started with an internship there. Through her experiences at Airbnb and elsewhere, Katie now designs the online interactions that enable activities which support the health of humans and our home, Earth
