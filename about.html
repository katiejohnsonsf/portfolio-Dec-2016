HI AGAIN,

In 2010 I was working as an analyst at 3Degrees — an environmental commodities trading firm. Having followed my interest in the environment to a degree in environmental science at Mills, I was frustrated with the structural barriers I faced within a company ostensibly oriented towards affecting structural change in the energy market.
When an Airbnb guest of my roommate’s engaged me in conversation in my kitchen one morning, I was awestruck from the experience of being shown a new world. I wanted others to have this experience.
I sought out companies that were enabling these kind of interactions. Airbnb had brought me the world-expanding conversation, so I started with an internship there. Through my experiences at Airbnb and elsewhere, I now design the online interactions that enable activities which support the health of humans and our home, Earth
