I guided the product strategy and scalable design strategy for launching the app and service in any neighborhood or city. The design process was a template in itself for customizing the initial content based on the needs of people in that area.


persona: After recruiting research subjects who were parents in San Francisco, I synthesized findings from interviews and surveys into a persona structured around Maslow's needs in a relational form (rather than hierarchy). The relationship-based model allowed us to hypothesize how the product might help support and facilitate local information sharing between the users' community stakeholders.
